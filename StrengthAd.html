Strength Training

Advanced



  • Equipment: Barbell, dumbbells, kettlebells
  • Exercises:
    • Barbell Back Squats – 5 sets of 5 reps
    • Deadlifts (Conventional or Sumo) – 5 sets of 5 reps
    • Weighted Pull-ups – 4 sets of 6 reps
    • Bench Press (Barbell or Dumbbells) – 4 sets of 6 reps
    • Kettlebell Swings – 3 sets of 20 reps
    • One-Arm Snatch (Dumbbell or Kettlebell) – 3 sets of 6 reps per arm
    • Hanging Windshield Wipers – 3 sets of 10 reps per side
  • Workout Plan:
    • Day 1: Heavy Squats + Upper Body (Pull-ups, Bench Press)
    • Day 2: Power Movements (Kettlebell Swings, Snatches)
    • Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery
    • Day 4: Deadlifts + Core (Windshield Wipers)
    • Day 5: Strength + Endurance (Farmer’s Carry, High-Rep Squats)
    • Day 6: Explosive Movements (Box Jumps, Snatches)
    • Day 7: Rest
