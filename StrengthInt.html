Strength Training

Intermediate



  • Equipment: Dumbbells, barbell, resistance bands
  • Exercises:
    • Barbell or Dumbbell Squats – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Deadlifts (Dumbbell or Barbell) – 4 sets of 8 reps
    • Pull-ups (Assisted if needed) – 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Overhead Barbell Press – 3 sets of 8 reps
    • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets of 8 reps per leg
    • Hanging Leg Raises – 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Farmer’s Carry – 3 rounds of 30 seconds
  • Workout Plan:
    • Day 1: Upper Body (Pull-ups, Shoulder Press, Rows)
    • Day 2: Lower Body (Squats, Deadlifts, Split Squats)
    • Day 3: Rest or active recovery
    • Day 4: Full-body (Deadlifts, Press, Core Work)
    • Day 5: Mobility + Cardio
    • Day 6: Lower Body Strength (Squats, Bulgarian Split Squats)
    • Day 7: Rest
