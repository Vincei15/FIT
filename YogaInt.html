Yoga Classes

Intermediate



  1. Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II) – Similar to Warrior I but with arms extended outward.
  2. Triangle Pose (Trikonasana) – Extend one leg out, reach one arm toward the ground, and the other toward the sky.
  3. Crow Pose (Bakasana) – Balance on hands with knees resting on upper arms.
  4. Boat Pose (Navasana) – Sit on the floor, lift legs, and balance on your tailbone.
  5. Half Moon Pose (Ardha Chandrasana) – Balance on one leg with the other lifted and one arm reaching to the ground.
  6. Camel Pose (Ustrasana) – Kneel and arch backward, reaching for heels.
  7. Seated Twist (Ardha Matsyendrasana) – Sit with one leg bent over the other and twist the torso.
