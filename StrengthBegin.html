Strength Training

Beginner



  • Equipment: Bodyweight, dumbbells
  • Exercises:
    • Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Push-ups (knee or full) – 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Glute Bridges – 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Bent-Over Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Plank – 3 rounds of 30-60 seconds
    • Lunges – 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Workout Plan:
    • Day 1: Full-body (Squats, Rows, Push-ups, Plank)
    • Day 2: Rest or light cardio
    • Day 3: Lower body (Lunges, Glute Bridges) + Core (Plank)
    • Day 4: Rest
    • Day 5: Upper body (Shoulder Press, Rows, Push-ups)
    • Day 6: Light cardio or active recovery
    • Day 7: Rest
