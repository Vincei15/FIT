Yoga Classes

Advanced



  1. Handstand (Adho Mukha Vrksasana) – Balance upside down on your hands.
  2. Forearm Stand (Pincha Mayurasana) – Like a handstand but on forearms.
  3. Scorpion Pose (Vrschikasana) – In a forearm stand, bend your back and bring feet toward your head.
  4. Firefly Pose (Tittibhasana) – Lift both legs in front of you while balancing on hands.
  5. King Pigeon Pose (Eka Pada Rajakapotasana) – Deep backbend with one leg folded in front and the other reaching overhead.
  6. Peacock Pose (Mayurasana) – Balance on hands with the body parallel to the ground.
  7. Lotus Headstand (Padmasana Sirsasana) – Perform a headstand with legs in lotus position.
