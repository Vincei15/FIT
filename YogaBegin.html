Yoga Classes

Beginner



  1. Mountain Pose (Tadasana) – Stand tall, feet together, arms by your side, and focus on posture and breath.
  2. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana) – Hands and feet on the mat, hips lifted, forming an inverted "V" shape.
  3. Child’s Pose (Balasana) – Knees wide, big toes touching, stretch arms forward and rest forehead on the mat.
  4. Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana) – Alternate between rounding and arching your back while on hands and knees.
  5. Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I) – Step one foot forward into a lunge, arms raised overhead.
  6. Tree Pose (Vrikshasana) – Stand on one leg with the other foot resting on the inner thigh or calf.
  7. Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana) – Sit with legs straight and fold forward, reaching for toes.
